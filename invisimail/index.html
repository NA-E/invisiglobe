InvisiMail focuses on high-stakes workplace communication where outcomes can affect income, job security, or career progression. It addresses the invisible effort, risk, and power imbalance involved in writing messages for situations such as performance reviews, HR disputes, accommodation requests, and termination responses.

By supporting clearer, safer, and more intentional communication, InvisiMail helps make invisible human effort visible and measurable within real workplace contexts.
