What InvisiGlobe Is

InvisiGlobe is building an Impact Operating System that makes invisible contributions visible, measurable, and trustworthy.

We help organizations:

  • Track real-world social and environmental activity
  • Measure impact using clear, explainable criteria
  • Publicly show where money goes and what it creates
