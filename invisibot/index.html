InvisiBot explores when, where, and how automated systems and AI should act on behalf of humans. It focuses on responsibility, boundaries, trust, and the human impact of delegating actions to machines.

By examining public perspectives on automation and agency, InvisiBot helps inform how human-centered systems should be designed before decisions or actions are handed over to technology.

This survey is for understanding views on automation, agency, and responsibility only. It does not deploy bots or automated actions.
